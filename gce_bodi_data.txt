The college has permanent campus, spread across 10.06 acres, was inaugurated by the Honourable Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu on 12-06-2015. Since its inception, the college has been dedicated to providing high-quality technical education, with an emphasis on engineering disciplines. The institution follows the curriculum prescribed by Anna University, ensuring that students receive a modern and relevant education that aligns with industry standards. The college is situated in Bodinayakanur, a town in Theni district known for its scenic landscape and proximity to the Western Ghats, the college benefits from a serene environment conducive to learning. Initially, the college offered five undergraduate programmes. At present, it offers six undergraduate programmes. Civil Engineering Mechanical Engineering Electrical and Electronics Engineering Electronics and Communication Engineering Computer Science and Engineering Computer Science and Engineering(Data Science) Each of these courses, approved by AICTE, has an annual intake of 60 students. Over the years, the college has developed a well-equipped campus with modern laboratories, a central library and essential student services. It places importance on the holistic development of students through various extracurricular activities, including cultural and technical fests. The institution emphasizes the development of its students for the job market, offering support through placement cells and industry collaborations. The college serves as a bridge between rural students and employment opportunities in urban and international markets. Through its educational offerings and its strategic focus on rural development, the Government College of Engineering, Bodinayakanur has become a pivotal institution for aspiring engineers in the Theni region and beyond. VISION To be global, vibrant and innovative center for Technical Education, to equip students with knowledge and skills in Engineering, expose hidden talents, opportunities to realize their full potential, inculcate national and human values and thus shape them into future professional engineers, entrepreneurs and above all good human beings. MISSION The Mission of the college is to contribute to society through promotion of teaching, learning and knowledge by developing the personality of students in a holistic manner by combining skills and values and by assimilating global development in education and adopting the latest technology.
